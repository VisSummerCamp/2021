About

Dates: August 3-6, 2021
Location: Minneapolis, MN
House Info: We have a giant house in the city (https://www.vrbo.com/2225233)

Logistics

Travel to Minneapolis, MN: We recommend that you arrive on August 3rd (Tuesday), any time in the afternoon (we don't know when the check-in time is, but we're guessing some time around 2pm). You should plan on leaving on the 6th (Friday), also some time in the morning or early afternoon. The general schedule is that we'll have a social meet-and-greet on Tuesday. Wednesday and Thursday are full workshop days. On Friday we'll use the morning to clean up and checkout (we assume check-out is around noon).

Travel to camp house: On your own

Rooming Situation: There should be a room for everyone.

Schedule

"Sessions" approximately distribute time into 3 chunks (AA,BB,CC)

  • - AA minutes of "listen" (presentation and/or introduction)
  • - BB minutes of "talk" (discussion split in blocks of breakout groups and discussion rejoinder)
  • - CC minutes of "social" (breaks, unstructured talking, snacking, stretching, resting)

In addition, you will decide the content for Wednesday's Unconf. Bring ideas!
